The GSC Basketball Team is a competitive basketball organization consisting of girls from the Faulkner County area and other cities throughout Arkansas. Our goal is to showcase the talent and resilience of the best basketball players in the country and increase their likelihood of earning an athletic scholarship. We will focus on fundamental training and development. The GSC is open to participation by all young women entering the 7th through 9th grade in the 2022-2023 Academic School Year.
