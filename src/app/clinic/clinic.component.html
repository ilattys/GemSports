Annual Girls Basketball Clinic

The Gem Sports Club presents its 1st Annual Girls Elite Basketball Skills Clinic. This event is to be held at Conway High Old Gym, 2300 Prince St., Conway, Ar 72034. The Elite Basketball Skills Clinic is now open for young girls ages 12-17 who aspire to play at the collegiate level. We give campers and parents the truth on what it takes to make it to the next level and be a true student-athlete. Our focus will be fundamentals, skills, athleticism, and mentality that will help you no matter what team or coach you play for. Campers should arrive early with a positive attitude and serious work ethic!
